- Logo treatment is oversized and horizontal: bronze monogram plus dark serif wordmark.
- Background is a warm ivory radial wash with low-contrast circular guide lines.
- Primary accent is deep medical green; secondary accent is bronze/gold for arrows and icon outlines.
- Component edges are soft, polished, and low-shadow; avoid colorful gradients or generic app cards.
